## üì± Social Media Formats

- Format: JPEG or MP4

- Resolution: 3840x2160 (4K)

### Color Grading:
```
Highlights: +10
Shadows: -5
Whites: +15
Blacks: -10
Clarity: +20
Vibrance: +15
Saturation: +5
Temperature: +5 (warmer)

```
